About Us

NU CAB service, Just for you.

Nucab, NIIT University's first and only transport service, integrates city transportation for customers and driver partners onto a technology platform.

As one of fastest growing cab services we ensure convenient, transparent and quick service fulfilment using technology to make transportation hassle free for everyone.

We, atNucab, are extremely paranoid about the security of our customer's information. We always appreciate your efforts to keep our customers safe. We have taken a small step towards the same, to safeguard our customer's data and give all the deserved credits to our dedicated researchers. We would be glad to invite you to participate in our bug bounty program. Please report any security vulnerabilities at Rinkal.singh@st.niituniversity.in.
